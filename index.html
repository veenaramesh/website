About

Hello! My name is Veena Ramesh. I am a 22-year old student at the University of Virginia, studying Computer Science and Cognitive Science, with a concentration in Linguistics.

Ever since I was little, I was interested in a seemingly unrelated group of things: philosophy, writing, psychology + neuroscience, languages, etc. And finally, at University, through my major, I have the ability to understand each of these topics through the lens of technology. Throughout these last four years, I have attempted to do just that: understand the world through technology and use technology to help the world around me.
